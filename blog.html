Top Five Most Underrated Queen Songs

18 July 2025

Sure, you may know Don’t Stop Me Now or Another Bites The Dust, but! What about these songs? I’ve been a fan of Queen since I was about 5, so I know a lot of their songs. Ready?

  1. Death On Two Legs (Dedicated to...): I think this was for their manager or something, but I'm not sure. But if you ever REALLY dislike someone, anonymously (or not) give them the link to this song!
  2. Face It Alone: For whatever reason, this isn't at all very famous. I know it was thought lost forever, and then it was found in 2022, but why has it not been very popular recently?
  3. I'm Love With My Car: There are plenty of people who really like cars and they probably are getting more of the technical references then I am, but it's still funny. It's the only song written entirely by Roger Taylor (that's the drummer, for those of you who don't know), and it's even sung by him (I didn't know that when I heard it and I freaked out when it didn't sound like Mercury).
  4. Ogre Battle: Probably no one would think Queen would do something like that, but yeah, they did. It's about ogres...battling. Okay. It should be in Shrek is all I'm saying.
  5. Headlong:You might know this song already if you listen to them a lot, but did you know the music video was the last music video of Mercury in color ever? It was actually gonna be part of May’s solo album, but he decided to let him sing it instead. And it’s awesome.

Hope you liked this little list, tell me if you can if you know any other underrated ones!

Welcome to my site

12 July 2025

Hey! You're here, finally! This is the blog of someone who prefers to remain anonymous. Unless I give you this link, whereupon I am not anonymous and I am your friend. And thus you know who I am. Obviously. But so that if anybody else stumbles upon this site, I am anonymous. An anonymous entity who probably isn't human, but does indeed (sadly) look like one. I'm not the first alien to look human, though. Can you name a few? I can list several from movies, tv shows, even songs. But alas, I can do nothing they can do (fly spaceships, phone home (I assure you I can't even remember my own phone number), be logical, play guitar, play 3D chess (kudos to those fans of that series who got the reference, you are in good hands here), or fight with a lightsaber). I can only sit here in my bedroom, overheating, and trying to connect my headphones so I listen to what my parents are calling rock and roll, but is actually just Brian Eno. I listen to a lot of things. Will tell my favourite bands and singers in an upcoming post, but it is no easy task.

Anyways, I'm creating this blog because I'm (a) running out of space in my diary and don't even start with buying a new one because I have a severe notebook-buying obsession and once I start I won't stop and (b), I kinda think it would be fun to blog without confining it to things like fashion, travel, music, cooking, etc. But no worries! All those things will be covered, just I won't do only that like some blogs do. It's gonna be personal and broad in range. It's something you'll pick up over your coffee break to see if I'm still alive and if I will continue to be for a little while more. And I'll write it between coffee breaks too! Or less coffee breaks, as I'll be pretty busy over the summer, and more over lunch or after camp, if I have time. Won't be too regular. Sorry. There's plenty of other websites and blogs to check out. More interesting than mine probably. Or go get a hobby. I'm learning Latin (okay, wait, that's not my hobby, that's my studies), reviewing music in my cute little notebook I bought obsessively and justified buying it by filling it with observations of the 4/4 beat and similarities to other musicians, sleeping (wait, that's not a hobby either, who's in charge of this), writing songs, drawing animals, and arguing with Chat-GPT over a obscure fact that I know because I'm weird and also I read a lot.

So, uh, that's all for now. If you know me and can contact me, just tell me what I should blog about next!
